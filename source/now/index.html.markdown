# What I'm doing now

<em class="date">This is a [Now page](https://nownownow.com/about). This page can also always be found at the permanent URL in the [Now page directory](https://nownownow.com/p/ZMe5).

## Work


I am in my last term for my Masters of Arts program in Psychedelics and Consciousness Studies at the University of Ottawa.

I did my fieldwork with Yage (Ayahuasca) near Sasaima, Colombia. Now I'm working on my practicum with [Psychedelia-Stiftung](https://psychedelia-stiftung.de) and graduating in July this year.

I recently started getting involved with [Psymposia](https://psymposia.com), the only organization I'm aware of doing actual autocritique in the field of psychedelic studies.

I'm working on [Mindscape Collective](https://mindscapecollective.org), the world's largest database of research on psychedelics, meditation, dreaming, and consciousness exploration. It's live, and updated periodically. I have ambitions for it that should manifest when I'm ready.

## Self

I'm happy at home in SLC with family and community. Had an amazing 6 weeks in Asia 6 months ago, and will be heading back to Europe for a month this summer.

Camping, video gaming, fun technical projects, baking bread, and facing the hellish reality we live in head-on with absurdity and humor has been keeping me relaxed lately.
